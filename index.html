About Us

Gembody Ltd develops intelligent ultrasound solutions for remote healthcare and preventive diagnostics. Our mission is to democratize access to early vascular and soft-tissue assessment through portable, AI-integrated platforms.

SonicVein™ – Remote Diagnostic Platform

SonicVein™ is an AI-powered software platform for remote screening and early diagnosis of varicose veins. It processes raw ultrasound signals using deep learning to provide non-invasive, interpretable insights—ideal for primary care or telehealth use.

GEMView™ – Portable AI Ultrasound Scanner

GEMView™ is a handheld, AI-enabled ultrasound device built for field use and remote clinics. It delivers real-time diagnostic analysis without requiring full imaging, enabling fast, low-cost healthcare deployment anywhere.
