Our Products

SonicVei™ – Remote Diagnostic Platform

SonicVein™ is an AI-powered software platform designed for remote screening and early-stage diagnosis of varicose veins. By analyzing ultrasound signals with proprietary machine learning algorithms, it identifies vascular abnormalities non-invasively and provides interpretable diagnostic insights. The system supports remote operation, enabling access to venous health assessment in primary care or underserved settings.

GEMSense™ – Wearable Ultrasound Patch

Continuous vascular and musculoskeletal monitoring.

GEMView™ – Portable AI Ultrasound Scanner

Handheld, AI-powered ultrasound for remote clinics and field use.
